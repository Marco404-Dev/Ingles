# Exercises: Negation and Questions

## Part 1: Change to Negative Form

Transform the following sentences into **negative sentences**.

1. She likes coffee.  
2. They are at school.  
3. He plays the guitar.  
4. We were late yesterday.  
5. I understand the lesson.  
6. She was happy with the result.  
7. They work on Sundays.  
8. He finished the project.
