# 1. Negation in English (Forma negativa)

En inglés, la negación se forma **usando auxiliares**, no cambiando el verbo principal como en español.

---

### 1.1 Negation with **BE** (am / is / are / was / were)

Se coloca **NOT** después del verbo **be**.

#### Structure:
Subject + BE + NOT + Complement

#### Examples:
- I am **not** tired.
- She is **not** at home.
- They are **not** students.
- He was **not** happy.
- We were **not** late.

También se pueden usar contracciones:
- is not → **isn't**
- are not → **aren't**
- was not → **wasn't**
- were not → **weren't**

---



# 2. Questions in English (Forma interrogativa)

En inglés, las preguntas se forman **invirtiendo el orden** del sujeto y el auxiliar.

---

### 2.1 Yes / No Questions with **BE**

#### Structure:
BE + Subject + Complement ?

#### Examples:
- **Are** you ready?
- **Is** she your sister?
- **Was** he angry?
- **Were** they at school?
