# 1. Negation in English (Forma negativa)

En inglés, la negación se forma **usando auxiliares**, no cambiando el verbo principal como en español.

---

### 1.1 Negation with **BE** (am / is / are / was / were)

Se coloca **NOT** después del verbo **be**.

#### Structure:
Subject + BE + NOT + Complement

#### Examples:
- I am **not** tired.
- She is **not** at home.
- They are **not** students.
- He was **not** happy.
- We were **not** late.

También se pueden usar contracciones:
- is not → **isn't**
- are not → **aren't**
- was not → **wasn't**
- were not → **weren't**

---


# Contraction

- She is not happy. / She isn't happy.
- They are not here. / They aren't here.
- He was not late. / He wasn't late.

- Is he not ready? / Isn't he ready?
- Are they not here? / Aren't they here?
- Is she not late? / Isn't she late?
