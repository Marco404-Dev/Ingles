# Ejercicios: Estructuras Modales y de Intención

## 1. Completa las oraciones con la estructura correcta

1. I ___ swim very well. (habilidad)  
2. She ___ finish her homework tonight. (necesidad)  
3. We ___ visit our grandparents tomorrow. (plan)  
4. He ___ go to the gym more often. (consejo)  
5. I ___ travel to Japan next year. (deseo formal)  
6. You ___ be careful when crossing the street. (consejo)  
7. I ___ call my friend later. (intención futura)  
8. They ___ finish the project by Friday. (obligación fuerte)  
9. I ___ study more for the exam. (recomendación)  
10. She ___ visit the museum yesterday. (posibilidad)  

---

## 2. Forma preguntas con la estructura correcta

11. ___ you help me with my homework? (habilidad)  
12. ___ he go to the party tonight? (intención futura)  
13. ___ we see that movie this weekend? (posibilidad)  
14. ___ she finish the report today? (necesidad)  
15. ___ they travel to Europe next summer? (deseo)  

---

## 3. Forma oraciones negativas con la estructura correcta

16. I ___ (not/can) speak French.  
17. She ___ (not/need to) go to work today.  
18. We ___ (not/plan to) visit them this week.  
19. He ___ (not/must) forget his homework.  
20. They ___ (not/want to) eat pizza tonight.  

---

## Respuestas

### 1. Completa las oraciones

2. need to  
3. are going to
