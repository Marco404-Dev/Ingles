# Ejercicios: Pronombres Objetos en Inglés

## 1. Completa las oraciones con el pronombre objeto correcto

1. Can you help ___? (yo)  
2. I saw ___ at the park. (ella)  
3. He invited ___ to the party. (nosotros)  
4. She is talking to ___ now. (tú)  
5. I will tell ___ the truth. (ellos)  
6. Do you like ___? (él)  
7. We will call ___ later. (usted)  
8. I love ___ very much. (ella)  
9. This gift is for ___? (yo)  
10. She gave ___ a book. (nosotros)  

---
