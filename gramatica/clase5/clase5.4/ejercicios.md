# Ejercicios: Negaciones en Inglés – don’t, doesn’t y didn’t

## 1. Completa las oraciones con don’t o doesn’t (presente)

1. I ___ like broccoli.  
2. She ___ play the guitar.  
3. They ___ watch TV every day.  
4. He ___ go to the gym on Mondays.  
5. We ___ eat meat.  
6. You ___ study English on weekends.  
7. It ___ rain much in summer.  
8. My parents ___ drink coffee.  
9. The dog ___ like loud noises.  
10. She ___ enjoy spicy food.  

---

## 2. Completa las oraciones con didn’t (pasado)

11. I (not/go) _______ to the park yesterday.  
12. He (not/watch) _______ the movie last night.  
13. They (not/play) _______ football last weekend.  
14. She (not/call) _______ her friend yesterday.  
15. We (not/finish) _______ the homework on time.  
16. You (not/take) _______ the bus to school.  
17. It (not/rain) _______ yesterday.  
18. My brother (not/eat) _______ breakfast this morning.  
19. They (not/visit) _______ their grandparents last week.  
20. She (not/read) _______ the book last night.  

---
