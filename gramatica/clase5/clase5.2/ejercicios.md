# Ejercicios: Verbos Regulares e Irregulares

## 1. Completa con el pasado simple (regular o irregular)

1. I (work) _______ yesterday.  
2. She (play) _______ tennis last weekend.  
3. We (go) _______ to the park yesterday.  
4. They (watch) _______ a movie last night.  
5. He (eat) _______ pizza for lunch.  
6. I (study) _______ for the test.  
7. She (write) _______ a letter to her friend.  
8. We (clean) _______ the house last Saturday.  
9. He (take) _______ the bus to school.  
10. They (love) _______ the concert.  

## 2. Completa con el participio pasado (regular o irregular)

11. I have (see) _______ that movie before.  
12. She has (eat) _______ breakfast already.  
13. We have (play) _______ football many times.  
14. He has (write) _______ three emails today.  
15. They have (go) _______ to Paris.  
16. I have (study) _______ English for two years.  
17. She has (watch) _______ all the episodes.  
18. We have (take) _______ the wrong bus.  
19. He has (clean) _______ his room.  
20. They have (love) _______ that book.
