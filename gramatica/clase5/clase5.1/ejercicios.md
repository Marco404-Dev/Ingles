# Ejercicios: DO y DID

## 1. Completa las preguntas (10 ejercicios)

1. ___ you like chocolate?  
2. ___ she play the guitar?  
3. ___ they go to the park yesterday?  
4. ___ he enjoy reading books?  
5. ___ we have a meeting today?  
6. ___ I need to call my friend?  
7. ___ your parents live in Lima?  
8. ___ it rain yesterday?  
9. ___ she know the answer?  
10. ___ you watch the movie last night?

---

## 2. Forma oraciones negativas (5 ejercicios)

11. I (not/watch) TV yesterday.  
12. He (not/like) pizza.  
13. They (not/study) English today.  
14. She (not/call) her mother last night.  
15. We (not/play) football on Sundays.

---

## 3. Da énfasis (5 ejercicios)

16. I (do/enjoy) learning English.  
17. She (did/call) her friend yesterday.  
18. They (do/work) hard every day.  
19. He (does/enjoy) playing tennis.  
20. We (did/finish) the project on time.

---
