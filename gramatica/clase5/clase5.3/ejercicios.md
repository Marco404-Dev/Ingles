# Ejercicios: DID en Inglés

## 1. Completa las preguntas usando DID

1. ___ you go to the park yesterday?  
2. ___ she finish her homework last night?  
3. ___ they watch the new movie?  
4. ___ he call his friend yesterday?  
5. ___ we see that show on TV?  
6. ___ I study for the test?  
7. ___ your parents visit you last weekend?  
8. ___ it rain yesterday?  
9. ___ she enjoy the party?  
10. ___ you take the bus to school yesterday?

---

## 2. Forma oraciones negativas usando DID

11. I (not/eat) _______ breakfast yesterday.  
12. He (not/go) _______ to the gym last week.  
13. They (not/watch) _______ TV last night.  
14. She (not/call) _______ her mother yesterday.  
15. We (not/finish) _______ the project on time.

---

## 3. Da énfasis usando DID

16. I (did/visit) _______ my grandparents last weekend.  
17. She (did/finish) _______ her homework before dinner.  
18. They (did/see) _______ that movie last night.  
19. He (did/take) _______ the bus to school.  
20. We (did/enjoy) _______ the concert yesterday.

---

## Respuestas

### 1. Preguntas
1. Did you go to the park yesterday?  
2. Did she finish her homework last night?  
3. Did they watch the new movie?  
4. Did he call his friend yesterday?  
5. Did we see that show on TV?  
6. Did I study for the test?  
7. Did your parents visit you last weekend?  
8. Did it rain yesterday?  
9. Did she enjoy the party?  
10. Did you take the bus to school yesterday?

### 2. Negaciones
11. I didn’t eat breakfast yesterday.  
12. He didn’t go to the gym last week.  
13. They didn’t watch TV last night.  
14. She didn’t call her mother yesterday.  
15. We didn’t finish the project on time.

### 3. Énfasis
16. I did visit my grandparents last weekend.  
17. She did finish her homework before dinner.  
18. They did see that movie last night.  
19. He did take the bus to school.  
20. We did enjoy the concert yesterday.
